Spaghetti Bolognes

Rice, Teasty, and Easy to cook!

Preps

15 mins

Cook

30 mins

Serving

4

Level

Easy

ingredients

  • 200g Spaghetti
  • 300g ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, mincel
  • 400g canned tomatoes
  • Salt & pepper

instructions

  1. Cook the Spaghetti according to the package instructions.
  2. Saute onion and gerlic, add groug beef.
  3. Stir in tomatoes and simmer for 20 minutes
